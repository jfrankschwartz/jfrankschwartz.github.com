Hello!

My name is Frank and I am an LMT in NE (incall) and greater (outcall) Portland. Doing bodywork is my profession and passion! Seeing someone leave feeling better than when they came in makes my job very rewarding. My approach to bodywork is holistic, regarding the body-mind as a whole unit and addressing each person where they are at that day. My work will benefit you whether you work in an office or are an athlete. You will have my undivided attention!

After being encouraged by some friends who were massage therapists, I enrolled locally at East-West College of the Healing Arts. A year and a half later I graduated, having completed 1002 hours of training. During my time there I studied a wide variety of modalities:

  • Cranio-Sacral
  • Deep Tissue
  • Myofascial
  • Sports Massage
  • Swedish
  • Thai
  • Trigger Point
  • Tui-Na

My current work draws from these and is evolving into a deeper style based on rhythms of the body.

I have referrals for practitioners of other disciplines as well for complementary care. My studio is located on the Woodlawn/Dekum Triangle, across the street from Firehouse restaurant. It is comfortable, spacious and quiet and there is usually ample street parking. I have no set office hours, so please inquire for an appointment. Scheduling in advance is encouraged but same day appointments are sometimes available. I hope to hear from you!

LGBTQA & H friendly—all types and stripes are welcome!

Anxiety, Depression, & Trauma Therapy

Areas of stress and trauma are often stored in the body's memory. Bringing these to the surface can help release them, and you, into a new place. It's ok to ask for help!

Corrective

I practice structural balancing work, incorporating all of the modalities I have studied. This is to help mitigate or reverse the effects of repetitive patterns that cause pain or limited ability.

Couples

I also provide tandem couples' massage. With enough notice, I can offer myself plus another male or female LMT to massage you and your partner at the same time. Or if you prefer, you can each relax on your own tables while I take turns working on each of you. Makes for a perfect date!

Relaxation

I provide a safe space for relaxation massage. Often relaxing the mind is the most powerful way to relax the body. My table is heated to keep you cozy and warm while the stress melts away!

Thai

Thai massage is sometimes described as "passive yoga", involving lots of stretching and compression. My athletic clients love this style. I can think of nothing better to get your chi flowing!
