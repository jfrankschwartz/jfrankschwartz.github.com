J. Frank Schwartz

1981 – 2013

Jeremy “Frank” Schwartz was found at his home in Portland, OR on December 15th, 2013 after unexpectedly passing. Frank was born on November 16th, 1981 to Jay & Donna Schwartz of Brandon, SD. He attended the East-West College of the Healing Arts in Portland, OR & graduated as a Licensed Massage Therapist. He deeply loved learning & enjoyed investing extra time in learning a wide variety of massages modalities.

Frank will be remembered for his simple & adventurous way of life. He had an intensely passionate but yet easygoing manner, a ready smirk & always a witty observation. He loved spontaneity, being independent & was fearless in his adventures! His traveling adventures always included his dear van, Eileen.

His profession was not really a profession, but a passion. He loved figuring out how to help people & their bodies function better…he loved the “why” & the “how” of fixing the human body. In his own words, “Doing bodywork is my profession and passion! Seeing someone leave feeling better than when they came in makes my job very rewarding.”

His interests included adventures in the wild outdoors, hiking & camping in the beautiful mountains, & weekend trips to the coast. He always had his camera ready for amazing intuitive shots & he would even make cooking a photographic story, which he loved sharing with family! His deeply introspective spirit loved reading philosophy, politics, history, & science—he craved knowledge which made his reading list never ending & eclectic. Frank used his deep philosophical & expressive nature to compose poems, lyrics, music & his observations of life.

Frank is loved by his surviving parents, Jay & Donna Schwartz, & four siblings: Joe (wife, Christina, daughter Ava) Schwartz, Jenna (husband, Ruben) Sanchez, Jane (husband, Jonathan) Grieme, & Jackson. Additionally his sweet Grandma, Hedy Swenson, & many loving Aunts, Uncles, Cousins & extended family.
